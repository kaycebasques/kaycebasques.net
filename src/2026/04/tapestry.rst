=============
Warp and woof
=============

Today I learned some tapestry jargon:

* The warp is the vertical threads that are
  held on the loom.

* The woof [#]_ is the horizontal threads that
  are inserted over and under the warp.

In a finished tapestry you usually can only see the
woof. The warp is an invisible foundation.

.. [#] A.k.a. the weft.
